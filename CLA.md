### How to accept

When you open your first pull request, the **CLA Assistant** bot will post a
link and ask you to confirm your agreement. Replying to confirm (or signing via
the linked page) records your acceptance against your GitHub identity and
applies to all of your future contributions — you only do this once.

Corporate contributors (where an employer owns the IP) may instead arrange a
signed corporate CLA by contacting the Project Owner.
